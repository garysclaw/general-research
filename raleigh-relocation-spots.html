Methodology

Drive Time Estimation

All drive times sourced from TravelMath.com and cross-referenced with Trippy.com — both based on fastest highway routes in normal (non-peak) traffic. Real-world times may vary ±15–30 min with traffic. Times represent city-center to city-center from Raleigh, NC.

Ski Area Criteria

Ski areas considered: Beech Mountain NC, Sugar Mountain NC, Appalachian Ski Mtn (Blowing Rock NC), Cataloochee NC, Ski Sapphire Valley NC, Winterplace WV, Snowshoe Mountain WV, Wintergreen VA, Massanutten VA, The Homestead VA. Threshold: ≤3 hours from candidate city.

Population Data

Metro population figures use Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) boundaries where available, sourced from US Census Bureau 2024 estimates and FRED economic data. City proper population is not used — MSA is the relevant unit for economic analysis.

Home Price Data

Median home prices sourced from Redfin, Zillow, and Norada Real Estate, reflecting 2025 market data. Ranges account for variation between sources and measurement periods (listing price vs. sale price). Treat as approximate ±10%.

Tech Job Scoring

Tech market rating (★ = 1–4) based on: presence of major tech/engineering employers, size of professional/scientific services sector, university R&D ecosystem, startup activity, and defense contractor presence. "Strong" = multiple Fortune 500 tech/eng employers or major university R&D anchor.

Map Radius Note

The dashed circle on the map represents an approximate 330-mile radius (~5 hours at highway speed). Actual drivable 5-hour isochrone is irregular due to mountain terrain, especially to the west and southwest of Raleigh where I-40 and US-74 limit speed.
